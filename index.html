Cuba Libre

Nicolás Guillén — translated by Langston Hughes & Ben Frederic Carruthers

The original Motivos de Son by Nicolás Guillén was published in 1930 and consisted of 8 poems. They were first published together in the Havana newspaper Diario de la Marina on April 20, 1930. The eight poems are:

  • "Negro bembón"
  • "Mi chiquita"
  • "Búcate plata"
  • "Sigue"
  • "Ayé me dijeron negro"
  • "Tú no sabe inglé"
  • "Si tú supiera"
  • "Mulata"

The following translation by Langston Hughes and Ben Frederic Carruthers was published by Anderson & Ritchie, 1948 with the title Cuba Libre. Our edition of the translation is based on the digital edition of the Hughes–Carruthers original published in December, 2001 by marxists.org. Hughes and Carruthers drew from various sources. Available sources for the original poems are listed in the Sources section, with the caveat that the editions may not match.
