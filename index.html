Cuba Libre

Nicolás Guillén — translations of Guillén's poems by Langston Hughes & Ben Frederic Carruthers

The following translation by Langston Hughes and Ben Frederic Carruthers was published by Anderson & Ritchie, 1948 with the title Cuba Libre. Our edition of the translation is based on the digital edition of the Hughes–Carruthers original published in December, 2001 by marxists.org. Hughes and Carruthers drew from various sources, and took liberties with the original texts, including translating portions of the poems instead of wholes. Available sources for the original poems are listed in the Sources section, with the caveat that the editions may not match.
